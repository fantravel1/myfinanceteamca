Common Questions

How does the team collaboration actually work?

Your team meets regularly to review your finances holistically. They share a secure workspace where they coordinate on your behalf. You get one point of contact while benefiting from multiple experts.

Can I start with just one service?

Absolutely. Many clients start with bookkeeping and add services as they grow. The beauty is that when you're ready for more support, your team already knows your business inside out.

How do you match experts to my business?

During discovery, we assess your industry, size, complexity, and goals. We then hand-pick team members with relevant experience who will work well together.

What if my needs change?

That's expected! We regularly review your service mix and adjust as needed. Scaling up for a funding round? We can add CFO hours. Things settling down? We can scale back.
